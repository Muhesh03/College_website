Electrical and electronic engineering

Electrical engineering is a branch of engineering that designs and develops electrical devices, including generators, motors, transformers, electronics, radio frequency systems, telecommunications, remote sensing, signal processing and instrumentation.

Electrical engineering is the field of engineering, concerned with the study, designing, and use of appliances and equipment which use any form of electricity. Therefore, it also includes electromagnetism.
Electrical engineering is a technical discipline concerned with the study, design, and application of electricity! Through electrical engineering, we can design devices and systems using electrical components such as resistors, capacitors, transistors, etc.
