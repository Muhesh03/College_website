The National Cadet Corps (Air Wing) of our Institution, affiliated to the 1 (TN) Air Squadron NCC, Tambaram, is a part of the Personality Development Program. The allotted strength for NCC is 50. Cadets (Boys and Girls) are enroled into the NCC program every year. These NCC cadets should compulsorily attend a 10-day camp in an academic year apart from other special camps viz., National Integration Camp, Vayu Sainik Camp, Air Force Attachment Camp etc., The cadets are given rigorous physical training which includes March Past, Range Firing, Skeet Shooting, Microlite Flying, Parasailing and so on. The NCC aims at developing character, comradeship, discipline, a secular outlook, the spirit of adventure and ideals of selfless service amongst young citizens. Further, it aims at creating a pool of organized, trained and motivated youth with leadership qualities in all walks of life, who will serve the Nation regardless of which career they choose. Needless to say, the NCC also provides an environment conducive to motivating young Indians to join the armed forces.
